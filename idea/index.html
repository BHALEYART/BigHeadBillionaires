what this actually is

You don't need
a business. You
need an idea.

The Idea Fair is a free 4-week event for anyone sitting on a product idea, app concept, brand, invention, or small business they haven't built yet. No pitch deck required to start. No funding required to join.

Across four weeks you'll sharpen the concept, get real feedback from peers and pros, learn how to market and pitch it, and submit a final version for review — for a shot at real funding and hands-on support to help you build it.

good to know

Questions, answered.

Do I need a finished business to join?

Nope. You need a strong idea and the willingness to sharpen it. Most people who join the Idea Fair start with something rough.

What kind of ideas count?

Products, apps, brands, services, inventions, or consumer-facing businesses — anything you'd want to see become real.

Is it free to enter?

Yes. Registering and participating in all four weeks is free.

What if I can't make every week?

Do what you can. X Spaces sessions are recorded, and links will be made available afterward so you can catch up on anything you miss.

How is the winner picked?

Final submissions are scored against six judging criteria — clarity, demand, market potential, execution plan, pitch quality, and creative potential.
