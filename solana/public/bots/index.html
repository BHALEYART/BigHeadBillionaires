STEP 2 — FUND BOT POOL required
🔐
One transaction. Two actions.
Clicking Send USDC to Bot fires a single atomic transaction signed by your wallet:
① 0.2 SOL → bot wallet (tx fees + token account rent)
② Creates bot's USDC token account (~0.002 SOL rent)
③ Sends your USDC to the isolated bot wallet

The bot keypair is generated entirely in your browser. Private keys are never sent to or stored on any server — they exist only in the .env file you download. Only you have them.
