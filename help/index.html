01 / Mission

Why we built this.

Most NFT projects are JPEGs and a Discord. BHB is a small, weird animation studio that decided to mint its cast on-chain and then build all the tools a cast needs to live: a customizer, an animator, an arcade, a marketplace, and a desktop suite. The art comes first. The chain is the ledger underneath.

Customizer V4
Free for anyone · Holders save traits on-chain · DUO Scenes
+

What it is

The Customizer is the browser-based character builder where every Big Head starts. Mix and match traits across Body, Outfit, Eyes, Mouth, Head, Texture, and Background to design a unique character. Free to use for anyone. Connect your wallet to load your BHB NFTs into the builder, pull their on-chain traits, and write new traits back to chain.

Modes

  • Solo — design one character in Slot 1. Standard 1000×1000 export.
  • DUO Scenes — two characters side-by-side. Slot 1 = Character 1 (left), Slot 2 = Character 2 (right, mirrored). 2000×1000 export, optimized for reaction scenes and animated clips.
  • Female Mode — toggle to switch the active character's base.

Key features

  • Trait sliders — cycle through every available trait in each category with ◀ ▶ arrows. Live preview on the canvas.
  • Trait list view — hit 📋 Show Trait List to see every trait selection at once.
  • Your BHB NFTs — connect a wallet and the builder lists every Big Head you hold. Pick one to operate on.
  • Load NFT Traits — pull the current on-chain traits from the selected NFT into the builder so you can edit from where it currently is.
  • Export PNG — free high-res download. No wallet needed.
  • Save to NFT — write your build on-chain. Costs 100,000 $BURG. Permanent.
  • Open in Animator — one-click handoff. The Animator pulls your saved character automatically.
  • Founder Pack traits — holders of a Founder Expansion Pack get 25 exclusive traits unlocked in the builder.

Step by step

  1. Open the Customizer. No wallet required to design. Assets load automatically.
  2. Pick a mode. Stay in Solo, or toggle 🎭 DUO Scenes for two characters.
  3. Use trait arrows to build. Click ◀ ▶ on each category. In DUO, use the 👤 Character 1 / 👤 Character 2 buttons to switch which character you're editing.
  4. (Optional) Connect wallet to load an NFT. Select one of your Big Heads from the list, then hit 📥 Load NFT Traits to pull its current traits into the builder.
  5. Export PNG or save to chain. 💾 Export PNG is free. ⛓ Save to NFT (100k BURG) writes your build to the NFT's metadata permanently.
  6. Send to Animator. Hit 🎬 Open in Animator → — the Animator picks up exactly what you built.
💡
DUO tip: in DUO mode, Character 2 is automatically mirrored. Design Character 1 first, then switch to Character 2 — they'll face each other in the final scene.
🔗
On-chain saves are permanent. Review your build before confirming. Metadata may take 12–24 hours to update inside your wallet display, but the change is on-chain immediately.
🔓
Founder Pack: connect a wallet holding a Founder Expansion Pack and 25 exclusive traits unlock automatically across the trait categories.

Animator V4
Auto lip sync · DUO Scenes · 88 backgrounds · H.264 MP4 export
+

What it is

The Animator brings your Big Head to life. Your character's body is locked from the Customizer — you build the look there, save it, then come to the Animator to animate Eyes & Mouth with audio. Supports solo and DUO (two-character) scenes. Exports H.264 MP4 directly in-browser via WebCodecs — no server, no upload.

⚠️
Build first. If the Animator shows "No Character Found," go to the Customizer, save a character, then come back. The Animator loads from whatever you last built.

Modes

  • Solo — animate one character. Standard frame.
  • DUO Scenes — 2000×1000 export. Character 1 on the left, Character 2 on the right (mirrored). Each character has its own independent Audio, Music, Background, Mouth, Eyes, and Volume timelines. Use the CH1 / CH2 switcher to choose which character you're editing.

The 6 timeline tracks

  • 🎧 Audio — primary character audio. Record live with 🎤, or 📁 upload an MP3/WAV. Per-track volume.
  • 🎵 Music — background music layered behind the character. Independent volume, fade-in, and fade-out controls.
  • 🖼 BG — three background modes: Character (from the Customizer build), Image (upload your own), Scenes (carousel of 88 preset backgrounds). In DUO mode, backgrounds fill the full 2000×1000 frame.
  • 👄 Mouth — Auto Lip Sync or manual mouth-shape keyframes (Closed/Mmm, Open/Ahh, Round/Ooo, Wide/Eee, Lips/Fff, L Sound, Th Sound).
  • 👁 Eyes — Auto Expressions with Mood Presets, or manual blinks + expression keyframes (Curious, Surprised, Joy, Infuriated, Ouchy, Stern).
  • 🔊 Volume — keyframe volume changes across the timeline with a step-hold curve.

Auto Lip Sync

Toggle 🤖 Auto Lip Sync ON and the mouth animates automatically from the audio's volume envelope. Two styles:

  • Ehh (default) — neutral wide-mouth shapes. Works for most speech.
  • Ooo (rounder) — rounder mouth shapes. Good for singing and emphatic delivery.

Auto Expressions

Expressions placed automatically based on audio volume and pauses. Pick a Mood Preset that matches your audio's emotion:

😡 Mad
Infuriated · Ouchy · Stern
😌 Calm
Curious · Stern · Stare
😄 Happy

😲 Shocked
Curious · Ouchy · Surprised

Hit 🔄 Regenerate Expressions to re-place them after changing audio or preset.

Step by step

  1. Build & save your character in the Customizer first. The Animator inherits the body and look from there.
  2. Open the Animator. Your character loads automatically. In DUO, both Character 1 and Character 2 load.
  3. Add audio. 🎤 Record live or 📁 Upload an audio file. Scrub the waveform to position the playhead.
  4. Enable Auto Lip Sync. Pick Ehh or Ooo sync style. Mouth animates automatically.
  5. Add expressions. Use ✨ Auto Expressions with a Mood Preset for instant results, or place eye keyframes manually at exact timestamps.
  6. Pick a background. Character (default), upload an Image, or pick from the 88 Scenes carousel. DUO fills 2000×1000.
  7. (Optional) Layer music. 📁 Upload Music, set volume and fade in/out durations.
  8. Export. Hit 🎥 Export Video. The Animator compresses to H.264 MP4 in-browser.

BHB Agent
Build a chat-ready AI character with a voice — Claude or GPT-4, ElevenLabs
+

What it is

BHB Agent turns a Big Head into a chat-ready AI character with its own look, voice, and personality. You bring your own API keys for Claude or GPT-4 (and ElevenLabs for voice), and the whole thing runs in your browser. Keys are stored locally — they never leave your machine.

🔑
You'll need API keys. The Agent is BYO (bring-your-own) credentials. Anthropic for Claude, OpenAI for GPT-4, ElevenLabs for voice. The links to get each are inside the Setup screen.

The 4 character tabs

  • 🎨 Customizer — design the character's look. Same trait system as the main Customizer. Female toggle supported. Founder Pack traits unlock when you connect a wallet that holds one.
  • 🧠 Soul — the personality engine. Set Identity (Name, Tagline, Archetype, Core Flaw), Personality (Speaking Style, Never Says, Always Says), Mood Preset (Mad / Calm / Happy / Shocked), and the Agent Prompt (Prompt Prefix + Backstory) that gets sent to the LLM.
  • 🎙 Voice — pair the character with an ElevenLabs voice. Browse voices by All / Male / Female, tune Stability, Similarity, Style, and Speaker Boost, and run a Test Voice line before locking it in.
  • 💬 Chat — once the character is saved, this is where the conversation happens. Voice playback toggles per message.

Step by step

  1. Open Agent Setup. Pick a provider — Claude (Anthropic) or GPT-4 (OpenAI).
  2. Paste your API key. Get one at console.anthropic.com or platform.openai.com. Optionally add an ElevenLabs key for voice.
  3. Hit Save & Launch. Keys are stored in your browser only.
  4. Create a new character. Click + NEW CHARACTER.
  5. Customizer tab — design the look. Same trait system as the main Customizer. Cycle traits, toggle Female if you want.
  6. Soul tab — write the personality. Fill in Identity, Personality, pick a Mood Preset, and write the Prompt Prefix + Backstory. This is what makes the character actually sound like itself.
  7. Voice tab (optional) — pick a voice. Browse the ElevenLabs library, tune the sliders, run a Test Voice. Default settings: Stability 0.45, Similarity 0.75, Style 0.30.
  8. Save Character. The character is added to your local roster.
  9. Chat. Open the Chat tab and start talking. Use the Voice toggle to hear responses out loud.

Higher stability = more consistent delivery. Higher similarity = closer to the source voice. Higher style = more expressive but less reliable. Speaker Boost adds clarity at a small cost in nuance.

06 / FAQ

Common questions.

The stuff people ask most often.

What is Big Head Billionaires, really?

A 100-character animated NFT project on Solana, and a stack of free browser tools built around it. Watch the animated series. Design a character. Animate one. Stream as one. Trade one. If you've held NFTs before, think of it like an art collection where the artist also shipped a whole production studio. If you haven't, just open the Customizer and start clicking arrows.

Can I use the Customizer and Animator without owning anything?

Yes. Both tools are free and don't require a wallet to design or export. You only need a wallet (and $BURG) if you want to save traits on-chain to a Big Head you own.

The collection is sold out. Can I still get one?

Yes — on the BHB Market. The 100 Big Heads are all owned by community members and you can buy one peer-to-peer, wallet-to-wallet. There will never be a new mint of the original 100.

What does $BURG do?

It's the on-chain fuel. You need it to save traits on your NFT, mint Expansion Packs, and unlock holder features. You earn it by burning eligible NFTs and by participating in Season Rewards. Full breakdown in the $BURG section above.

Is this an investment?

No. We don't promise returns and we won't pretend to. This is an art project that lives on-chain. Buy a Big Head because you want to use it, animate it, stream as it, and watch the series. Anything else is your own decision and your own risk.

What are Expansion Packs?

Limited NFT drops (25 supply each) that unlock new trait categories inside the Customizer for anyone who holds the pack. They cost 1,000,000 $BURG to mint. Existing Big Heads gain access to those traits — no new characters are minted. See Expansion Packs.

What wallet should I use?

Solflare is the recommended wallet — its in-app browser supports the full BHB tool suite on mobile and desktop. Phantom works for most actions but newly minted NFTs may not display correctly there until the collection is verified on-chain (and metadata can take 12–24 hours to update inside any wallet).

Are burns really irreversible?

Yes. Once you approve the transaction, the NFT moves to the burn wallet and cannot be recovered. Read the on-page warning before confirming. The $BURG payout lands in your wallet immediately on confirmation.

Can I commission animations or appear in episodes?

The animated series uses real community voices and holder Big Heads as cast members. The fastest way to get involved is to join the Discord and submit clips during Season Rewards.

Who's behind BHB?

Built and run by @BHaleyArt (founder, artist, animator, voice actor, primary dev) with @FitzyFever as creative co-lead, game dev, and background artist, and @MetaMike on AI/tech. Small team, big heads.
