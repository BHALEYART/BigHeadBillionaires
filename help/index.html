01 / Mission

Why we built this.

Most NFT projects are JPEGs and a Discord. BHB is a small, weird animation studio that decided to mint its cast on-chain and then build all the tools a cast needs to live: a customizer, an animator, an arcade, a marketplace, and a desktop suite. The art comes first. The chain is the ledger underneath.

01

Art first, chain second

Every character is hand-built. Every voice comes from a real person. The blockchain proves ownership; it isn't the product.

02

Tools, not promises

We don't pitch a roadmap. We ship a customizer, an animator, an arcade, a live-streaming plugin, and an AI agent studio. Holders get to use them.

100 forever

One hundred Big Heads exist. There will never be more. Expansion Packs add traits to the existing 100 — not new characters.

Customizer
Free for anyone · Holders save traits on-chain
+

What it is

A browser-based character builder. Mix and match traits across Eyes, Mouth, Head, Outfit, Texture, Body, and Backgrounds to design a unique Big Head. Free to use for anyone — connect your wallet to save traits directly onto your minted NFT.

Key features

  • Trait sliders — cycle through every trait in each category with ◀ ▶ arrows.
  • Female Mode — toggle to the female character base.
  • DUO Scenes — build two characters side-by-side. Export at 2000×1000.
  • Slot system — save up to 2 builds in browser slots.
  • Export PNG — high-res download, no wallet required.
  • Save to NFT (wallet) — write traits on-chain for 100,000 $BURG.
  • Load NFT Traits — pull current on-chain traits into the builder.
  • Open in Animator — one-click handoff with traits intact.

Step by step

  1. Open the Customizer. No wallet required to design. Assets load automatically.
  2. Use trait arrows to build your character. Click ◀ ▶ on each trait slot. Changes preview live.
  3. Save to a slot. Hit 💾 SAVE SLOT 1 or SLOT 2 to persist in localStorage.
  4. Export or save to chain. Download PNG free, or pay 100k $BURG to write traits to your NFT.
  5. Send to Animator. Click 🎬 Open in Animator → to bring your character over with its full trait set.
💡
DUO tip: DUO mode designs two characters at once — great for reaction scenes. Each has its own trait slots.
🔗
On-chain saves are permanent. Make sure you're happy with the design before confirming. Wallet display may take 12–24 hours to update.

Animator
Auto lip sync · DUO Scenes · H.264 MP4 export
+

What it is

The Animator brings your Big Head to life. Load a character built in the Customizer, attach audio, set expressions on a timeline, and export a polished animated video. Supports solo and DUO scenes.

Key features

  • Auto Lip Sync — mouth shape driven by audio volume. Ehh or Ooo sync styles.
  • Manual mouth keyframes — Closed/Mmm, Open/Ahh, Round/Ooo, Wide/Eee, Fff, L, Th at exact timestamps.
  • Auto Expressions — placed automatically by audio volume + pauses. Mood Presets: 😡 Mad, 😌 Calm, 😄 Happy, 😲 Shocked.
  • Eye keyframes — Curious, Surprised, Joy, Infuriated, Ouchy, Stern at any point.
  • Volume + music keyframes — automate volume; layer a music track with fades.
  • 58 background scenes — preset carousel, green screen, solid, or upload.
  • DUO Mode — two characters, independent audio + expressions, 2000×1000 export.
  • MP4 Export — H.264 + AAC via WebCodecs. In-browser. No server.

Getting started

  1. Build your character in the Customizer first. The Animator loads from saved slots. Hit 🎬 Open in Animator from there.
  2. Record or upload audio. 🎤 Record live, or 📁 upload. The waveform displays — tap to scrub.
  3. Enable Auto Lip Sync. Toggle 🤖 Auto Lip Sync ON. Mouth animates automatically during playback and export.
  4. Set expressions. ✨ Auto Expressions with a Mood Preset for quick results, or place eye keyframes manually.
  5. Pick a background. Select from the 58-preset carousel, upload your own, or use green screen.
  6. Export. Hit 🎥 Export Video. Compresses to H.264 MP4. On mobile, the Share sheet appears.
💡
Best results: record audio with minimal background noise. Auto Lip Sync performs best on clear speech. Match the Mood Preset to the emotion of your audio.
📱
Mobile users: the Animator uses Reduced UI on small screens. iOS export uses the Web Share API — tap the share icon when prompted to save to camera roll.
🎭
DUO tip: in DUO mode each character (CH1 / CH2) has its own audio and expression timeline. Use the CH1 / CH2 buttons to switch which one you're editing.

AI Agents
Drive the full Customizer → ElevenLabs → Animator → MP4 pipeline
+

What it is

AI agents (such as OpenClaw bots on MoltBook) can drive the full BHB character pipeline autonomously — building a character, generating a voice with ElevenLabs, animating it with lip sync, and exporting a finished MP4. Designed for computer-use / headless browser agents with minimal manual interaction.

Requirements

Browser
Chromium
Automation
Computer use
Voice
ElevenLabs

Agent Session API

Skip manual trait selection. POST traits, get pre-seeded Customizer and Animator URLs that auto-load on open.

Endpoint

POST https://bigheadbillionaires.com/api/agent-session

Request body

{
  "traits": {
    "BODY": "Dark Tone",
    "HEAD": "Outlaw",
    "EYES": "Grumpy",
    "MOUTH": "Mmm",
    "OUTFIT": "Trenches",
    "TEXTURE": "None",
    "BACKGROUND": "Graphite"
  },
  "female": false,
  "slot": 1
}

Response

{
  "customizerUrl": "https://bigheadbillionaires.com/customizer/?agent=eyJ...&slot=1",
  "animatorUrl":   "https://bigheadbillionaires.com/animator/?agent=eyJ...&slot=1",
  "indices": { "BODY": 11, "HEAD": 39, "EYES": 6, ... },
  "slot": 1
}

The 4-stage pipeline

  1. Customizer — build the character (browser). Use the Agent Session API for a pre-seeded URL, or cycle traits manually with ◀ ▶. Use "Open in Animator" to sync via localStorage.
  2. ElevenLabs — generate voice (API). Call the TTS API with the character's voice ID. Recommended: Stability 0.40–0.55, Similarity Boost 0.75.
  3. Animator — assemble & animate (browser, 2 clicks). Open the pre-seeded animatorUrl. Upload the .mp3 — lip sync and expressions fire automatically. Click Export.
  4. Export — H.264 MP4. Encoded in-browser via WebCodecs. No server upload.
⚠️
Browser automation required. Stages 1 and 3 use browser-based GUI tools, not APIs. Your agent needs computer use or headless browser control.
🎭
DUO Mode (desktop only): two-character scenes are supported. Each character gets its own audio, expression timeline, and lip sync.

Soul files & character schema

The repo includes a soul file format — structured JSON character definitions that specify traits, voice parameters, expression maps, and dialogue prompt prefixes. Start with example-char.json (blank template) and reference gravel-pete.json as a completed example.

06 / FAQ

Common questions.

The stuff people ask most often.

What is Big Head Billionaires, really?

A 100-character animated NFT project on Solana, and a stack of free browser tools built around it. Watch the animated series. Design a character. Animate one. Stream as one. Trade one. If you've held NFTs before, think of it like an art collection where the artist also shipped a whole production studio. If you haven't, just open the Customizer and start clicking arrows.

Can I use the Customizer and Animator without owning anything?

Yes. Both tools are free and don't require a wallet to design or export. You only need a wallet (and $BURG) if you want to save traits on-chain to a Big Head you own.

The collection is sold out. Can I still get one?

Yes — on the BHB Market. The 100 Big Heads are all owned by community members and you can buy one peer-to-peer, wallet-to-wallet. There will never be a new mint of the original 100.

What does $BURG do?

It's the on-chain fuel. You need it to save traits on your NFT, mint Expansion Packs, and unlock holder features. You earn it by burning eligible NFTs and by participating in Season Rewards. Full breakdown in the $BURG section above.

Is this an investment?

No. We don't promise returns and we won't pretend to. This is an art project that lives on-chain. Buy a Big Head because you want to use it, animate it, stream as it, and watch the series. Anything else is your own decision and your own risk.

What are Expansion Packs?

Limited NFT drops (25 supply each) that unlock new trait categories inside the Customizer for anyone who holds the pack. They cost 1,000,000 $BURG to mint. Existing Big Heads gain access to those traits — no new characters are minted. See Expansion Packs.

What wallet should I use?

Solflare is the recommended wallet — its in-app browser supports the full BHB tool suite on mobile and desktop. Phantom works for most actions but newly minted NFTs may not display correctly there until the collection is verified on-chain (and metadata can take 12–24 hours to update inside any wallet).

Are burns really irreversible?

Yes. Once you approve the transaction, the NFT moves to the burn wallet and cannot be recovered. Read the on-page warning before confirming. The $BURG payout lands in your wallet immediately on confirmation.

Can I commission animations or appear in episodes?

The animated series uses real community voices and holder Big Heads as cast members. The fastest way to get involved is to join the Discord and submit clips during Season Rewards.

Who's behind BHB?

Built and run by @BHaleyArt (founder, artist, animator, voice actor, primary dev) with @FitzyFever as creative co-lead, game dev, and background artist, and @MetaMike on AI/tech. Small team, big heads.
