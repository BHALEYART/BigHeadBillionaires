Play & Mint
Play to Mint
Score 30,000 pts in BHB Arcade → unlock mint for 5M BURG
▼

What is it?

Play to Mint is the gated entry point to Big Head Billionaires. You play the in-browser BHB Arcade game to hit a score target, which unlocks the ability to mint one of the 100 total BHB NFTs using BURG tokens.

How to mint — step by step

  1. 1
    Get BURG tokensYou need 5,000,000 BURG to mint. Buy on pump.fun — CA: 6disL...pump
  2. 2
    Connect your Solana walletPhantom, Solflare, or Ledger. Hit "Connect Wallet" in the nav.
  3. 3
    Play BHB ArcadeReach the 30,000 point target to unlock the mint button. Your progress bar fills as you score.
  4. 4
    Mint your BHBOnce unlocked, hit 🍔 MINT NOW. Costs 5,000,000 BURG + ~0.02 SOL network fee. Strictly 1 per wallet.
  5. 5
    Head to the CustomizerYour freshly minted BHB NFT is now ready to personalize.
Supply

Only 100 NFTs total exist, ever. No future mints.

Price

5,000,000 BURG + ~0.02 SOL gas fee (~$0.64).

Limit

1 NFT per wallet, strictly enforced on-chain.

💡

Mint is open! You don't need to hit the score — if mint is currently unlocked it will say "Mint available now" on the page. The arcade gate may be re-enabled at any time.

Customizer
Character Customizer
Design your Big Head — free for everyone. Save traits on-chain with your NFT.
▼

What is it?

The Customizer is a browser-based character builder. Mix and match traits across Eyes, Mouth, Head, Outfit, Texture, Body, and Backgrounds. Free to use for anyone — connect your wallet to save traits directly onto your minted NFT.

Key Features

  • Trait sliders — Cycle through every available trait with ◀ ▶ arrow controls.
  • Female Mode — Toggle to switch to the female character base.
  • DUO Scenes NEW — Build a two-character side-by-side scene, export at 2000×1000 px.
  • Slot system — Save up to 2 character builds in browser slots.
  • Export PNG — Download your character as a high-res PNG instantly. No wallet needed.
  • Save to NFT Wallet — Write traits on-chain to your BHB NFT. Costs 100,000 BURG.
  • Load NFT Traits — Pull on-chain traits from any BHB NFT in your wallet.
  • Open in Animator — Send your character directly to the Animator with one click.

Step-by-step

  1. 1
    Open the CustomizerNo wallet required to design. Assets load automatically.
  2. 2
    Use trait arrows to buildClick ◀ ▶ on each trait slot to cycle options. Changes preview live on the canvas.
  3. 3
    Save to a slotHit 💾 SAVE SLOT 1 or SLOT 2 to persist your build in localStorage.
  4. 4
    Export or save to chainDownload PNG for free, or connect wallet and pay 100k BURG to write traits to your NFT permanently.
  5. 5
    Send to AnimatorClick 🎬 Open in Animator → to bring your character into the Animator with its full trait set intact.
💡

DUO tip: Switch to DUO mode to design two characters simultaneously. Each character has its own trait slots.

🔗

On-chain saves: Costs 100,000 BURG and permanently updates the metadata on your token. Changes may take 12–24 hours to reflect inside your wallet.

Animator
Character Animator
Animate eyes & mouth with audio. Export as H.264 MP4. DUO Scenes supported.
▼

What is it?

The Animator brings your Big Head to life. Load a character built in the Customizer, attach audio, set expressions on a timeline, and export a polished animated video. It supports solo and DUO (two-character) scenes.

Key Features

  • Auto Lip Sync — Mouth shape driven automatically by audio volume. Choose Ehh or Ooo sync style.
  • Manual Mouth Keyframes — Set specific shapes at exact timestamps.
  • Auto Expressions — Expressions placed automatically based on audio volume & pauses. Pick a Mood Preset: 😡 Mad, 😌 Calm, 😄 Happy, 😲 Shocked.
  • Eye Keyframes — Manually add blinks and expressions at any point.
  • Volume Keyframes — Automate volume changes over time.
  • Music Track — Layer a separate background music track with fade in/out controls.
  • 88 Background Scenes — Preset scene backgrounds. Supports green screen, solid color, or image upload.
  • DUO Mode NEW — Two characters, independent audio/expressions, 2000×1000 export.
  • Export MP4 — H.264/AAC MP4 via WebCodecs. Internet-ready quality.

Getting started

  1. 1
    Build your character in the Customizer firstThe Animator loads character data from the Customizer's saved slots. Click 🎬 Open in Animator from there.
  2. 2
    Record or upload audioUse 🎤 Record to capture your voice live, or 📁 Upload a file.
  3. 3
    Enable Auto Lip SyncToggle 🤖 Auto Lip Sync ON. The mouth will animate from audio volume automatically.
  4. 4
    Set expressionsUse ✨ Auto Expressions with a Mood Preset for quick results, or manually place eye keyframes.
  5. 5
    Pick a backgroundSelect a scene from the preset carousel, upload your own image, or use green screen.
  6. 6
    Export your videoHit 🎥 Export Video. Compresses to H.264 MP4. On mobile: Share sheet or download overlay will appear.
💡

Best results: Record audio with minimal background noise. Auto Lip Sync performs best on clear speech.

📱

Mobile users: The Animator uses a Reduced UI mode on small screens. Export on iOS uses the Web Share API — tap the share icon when prompted.

🎭

DUO tip: Each character (CH1 / CH2) has its own independent audio track, lip sync, and expression timeline.

BHB Live
BHB Live — OBS Plugin
Stream your Big Head live. Real-time lip sync, expression hotkeys, green screen. Mac & Windows.
▼

What is it?

BHB Live is a desktop app that puts your Big Head Billionaire on screen as a live avatar during streams — similar to VTube Studio. It runs as a separate window that OBS captures. Your microphone drives real-time lip sync, hotkeys trigger eye expressions, and auto-blink fires on a timer. Works on Mac and Windows.

Key Features

  • Auto Lip Sync — Mic input drives mouth shapes in real time. Mouth opens and closes with your voice automatically.
  • Expression Hotkeys — Assign any key (F1–F12, letters, combos) to 8 expressions: Stare, Curious, Joy, Surprised, Stern, Infuriated, Ouchy, Blink. Keys fire globally — even when OBS is in focus.
  • Auto Blink — Randomized blink on a configurable interval (1–12s). Toggle on/off.
  • Background Modes — 🟩 Green Screen, 🟦 Blue Screen, ⬛ None, 🖼 Character BG layer, 🌆 88 Scene presets.
  • 4 Memory Slots — Save and name up to 4 full character builds. Push any slot to live instantly.
  • First-run asset download — Downloads all character assets once on first launch. Fully offline after that.

Download & Install

  1. 1
    Download the installer for your OS→ BHB Live Releases on GitHub
    Download BHB-Live-Mac (contains the .dmg) or BHB-Live-Windows (contains the .exe). Unzip the download first.
  2. 2
    InstallMac: Open the .dmg → drag BHB Live into Applications → right-click → Open → click Open in the security dialog (first launch only).
    Windows: Double-click the .exe → follow the installer → appears in Start Menu and Desktop.
  3. 3
    First launch — one-time asset downloadBHB Live downloads all character assets (~150 MB) and saves them to your device. This only happens once. Every launch after is instant with no internet required.
  4. 4
    Allow microphone access when promptedRequired for lip sync. On Mac: System Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone → enable BHB Live if it doesn't prompt automatically.

OBS Setup

  1. 1
    Build your character & GO LIVEDesign your Big Head, save to a slot, set your background mode, then click GO LIVE. The frameless canvas window opens — this is what OBS captures.
  2. 2
    Add to OBSAdd Source → Window Capture → select "BHB Live — OBS Capture". Add a Chroma Key filter if using green or blue screen.
  3. 3
    Assign expression hotkeysIn the right panel, click any expression's key slot and press your chosen key. Keys fire globally even when OBS is focused.
💡

Mac Gatekeeper: If the app won't open, go to System Settings → Privacy & Security → Allow Anyway. Or in Terminal: xattr -cr /Applications/BHB\ Live.app

📡

Pushing changes to live: After swapping characters or settings, hit ▶ PUSH STATE in the header to sync the control panel with the live canvas window instantly.

AI Agents
Connecting AI Agents to BHB Tools
Automate the full pipeline — Customizer → ElevenLabs → Animator → MP4 export.
▼

What is it?

AI agents (such as OpenClaw bots on MoltBook) can drive the full BHB character creation pipeline autonomously — building a character, generating a voice with ElevenLabs, animating it with lip sync, and exporting a finished MP4.

Requirements

Browser

Chromium-based recommended (Chrome, Edge, Brave). Firefox supported. Safari: limited export.

Browser Automation

Computer use or headless browser control required for Stages 1 and 3. ElevenLabs (Stage 2) is API-only.

ElevenLabs Account

Free tier is sufficient for short clips. Required for voice generation.

Audio Format

.mp3 or .wav — mono or stereo, any sample rate.

Agent Session API

POST your character traits and receive pre-seeded Customizer and Animator URLs — traits load automatically on open.

Endpoint
POST https://bigheadbillionaires.com/api/agent-session
Request body
{
  "traits": {
    "BODY": "Dark Tone",
    "HEAD": "Outlaw",
    "EYES": "Grumpy",
    "MOUTH": "Mmm",
    "OUTFIT": "Trenches",
    "TEXTURE": "None",
    "BACKGROUND": "Graphite"
  },
  "female": false,
  "slot": 1
}
Response
{
  "customizerUrl": "https://bigheadbillionaires.com/customizer/?agent=eyJ...&slot=1",
  "animatorUrl":   "https://bigheadbillionaires.com/animator/?agent=eyJ...&slot=1",
  "indices": { "BODY": 11, "HEAD": 39, "EYES": 6, ... },
  "slot": 1
}

Pipeline — 4 stages

  1. 1
    Customizer — Build the character (browser)Use the Agent Session API to get a pre-seeded Customizer URL, or manually cycle trait slots.
  2. 2
    ElevenLabs — Generate voice audio (API callable)Call the ElevenLabs TTS API. Recommended: Stability 0.40–0.55, Similarity Boost 0.75.
  3. 3
    Animator — Assemble and animate (browser — 2 clicks)Open the pre-seeded animatorUrl — traits, Auto Expressions, and mood preset load automatically. Upload the .mp3 — lip sync fires automatically. Click Export.
  4. 4
    Export — H.264 MP4 outputThe Animator encodes in-browser via WebCodecs (H.264 + AAC). No server upload.
⚠️

Browser automation required. Stages 1 and 3 use browser-based GUI tools — not APIs. Your agent must be capable of computer use or headless browser control.

🎭

DUO Mode (desktop only): Two-character scenes supported. Configure via the CH1 / CH2 switcher — each character has its own independent audio track, expression timeline, and lip sync.

Soul files & character schema

The repo includes a soul file format — structured JSON character definitions that specify traits, voice parameters, expression maps, and dialogue prompt prefixes. Start with example-char.json and reference gravel-pete.json as a completed example.
