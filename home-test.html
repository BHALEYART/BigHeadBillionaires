★ GOOD TO KNOW

Questions, answered.

Is it really free?
Yes. Designing a character, animating it, going live, and clipping videos are all free in your browser. There's nothing to install and no account to create.
Do I need to know anything about crypto or NFTs?
Nope. You can design, animate, stream, and export without ever touching a wallet. The crypto side is completely optional — it only matters if you later want to own a permanent on-chain version of your character.
What can I make with it?
A talking avatar for videos, a live VTuber-style character for streaming, reaction clips for TikTok and Reels, or just a fun personalized character to share with friends.
Does my video get uploaded anywhere?
No. The Animator and Clipper process everything locally on your device — your footage never leaves your computer.
What do I need to run it?
A modern browser — Google Chrome on a desktop works best for animating and clipping. A microphone helps for lip sync and going live.
