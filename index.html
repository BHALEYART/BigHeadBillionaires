How It Works
// Five steps from zero to animated NFT
1

Play the Game

Hit the score threshold in our browser game. The mint button appears when you earn it. No score, no button.

2

Mint Your NFT

Connect your Phantom wallet and pay 5,000,000 SPL tokens. One per wallet. 100 total. Your base character lands in your wallet on Solana.

3

Grab an Expansion Pack (Optional)

Visit Expansions to mint limited trait packs. 25 per pack. Holding the pack NFT unlocks those traits in your customizer.

4

Customize Your Character

Open the Customizer. Mix and match traits from your base set and any packs you hold. Finalize and save to chain.

5

Animate & Export

Take your finalized character into the Animator. Record audio, add lip sync, choose a background, export video.
