📖 How Season Rewards Work
  1. Connect your Solana wallet and sign in to register for the current season.
  2. Hold at least one Big Head Billionaires NFT in that wallet — required to register and submit.
  3. Submit up to 2 clips per week featuring your BHB character — 8 clips total over the season. Each clip earns 62,500 BURG.
  4. Pick a Quest when you submit. Season 2 has 20 quests — short prompts that shape what your clip is about. Use the Customizer, Animator, and Clipper to bring them to life.
  5. Submit all 8 for a perfect season: 500,000 BURG total.
  6. 24-hour cooldown between submissions, and no more than 2 in any rolling 7-day window.
  7. Active for 28 days, then a 2-day display window for final results before the next season opens.
⚡ Season 2 is TikTok-only and every clip must complete one of the 20 Quests — pick yours when you submit.
